# HOTA — Heritage On The Air

## What is HOTA?

Heritage On The Air (HOTA) is a South African amateur radio programme promoting operating from heritage sites and cultural landmarks. The programme is expanding internationally and includes approximately 300 references.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the heritage site
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Region:** South Africa (expanding)
