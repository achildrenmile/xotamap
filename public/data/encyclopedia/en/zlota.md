# ZLOTA — ZL On The Air

## What is ZLOTA?

ZL On The Air (ZLOTA) is a New Zealand amateur radio programme covering various reference types including districts, regions, and special locations. The programme is operated through the ontheair.nz platform and has over 1,500 references.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the reference
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Region:** New Zealand
