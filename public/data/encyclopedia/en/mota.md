# MOTA — Mills On The Air

## What is MOTA?

Mills On The Air (MOTA) is an amateur radio programme promoting operating from mills and windmills. Coordinated through the GMA platform, it is particularly active on annual Mill Day events in Germany and internationally.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the mill
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Special:** Often combined with annual Mill Day events
