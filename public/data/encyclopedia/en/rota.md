# ROTA — Railways On The Air

## What is ROTA?

Railways On The Air (ROTA) is a British amateur radio programme promoting operating from heritage railways and historic railway stations. Typically held as an annual event weekend, it is organised by the Railway Fraternity Radio Group.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the railway/station
- **Equipment:** Any setup
- **Region:** UK / International
