# KOTA — Kids On The Air

## What is KOTA?

Kids On The Air (KOTA) is an amateur radio programme specifically for children aged 10 to 15. Originating in the Czech Republic, the programme takes place at youth camps. Licensed operators supervise the young participants at the radio station.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Equipment:** Supervised station at youth camps
- **Operating modes:** SSB, CW, FM
- **Special:** Participants are children aged 10–15, supervised by licensed operators.
