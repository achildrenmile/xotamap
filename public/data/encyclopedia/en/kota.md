# KOTA — Kids On The Air

## What is KOTA?

Kids On The Air (KOTA) is an amateur radio programme originating in the Czech Republic that aims to inspire children and young people aged 10 to 15 for amateur radio. The programme was created to provide young people with an accessible and fun introduction to radio technology.

Unlike most xOTA programmes, KOTA is not based on fixed geographic references. Instead, it is an activity-based programme with special events and contests organised for children to participate in under the supervision of licensed radio amateurs. The idea is to introduce young people to technology, communication and international understanding in a playful way.

KOTA makes an important contribution to youth development in amateur radio. In an era dominated by digital media, the programme offers children the chance to gain practical technical experience and connect with peers worldwide. Events take place regularly and are supervised by experienced radio amateurs.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** No fixed minimum; depends on the specific event
- **Activation Zone:** No fixed references; participation in organised KOTA events
- **Equipment:** Usually provided by supervising radio amateurs or clubs
- **Operating Modes:** All modes, with voice operation (SSB, FM) preferred for beginners
- **Special Requirements:** Participants must be between 10 and 15 years old; supervision by a licensed radio amateur is required

## Points & Awards

| Award | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| KOTA Participant Certificate | Participation in a KOTA event |
| KOTA Activist | Participation in 3 KOTA events |
| KOTA Chaser | 10 KOTA stations contacted |
| Special Diplomas | Available at specific KOTA events |

## Getting Started

1. Visit the KOTA website at hamradio.cz/kota/ and find out about upcoming events
2. Contact a local amateur radio club that supports KOTA activities
3. Participate in a KOTA event — under the guidance of a licensed radio amateur
4. Make your first QSOs and experience the fascination of amateur radio
