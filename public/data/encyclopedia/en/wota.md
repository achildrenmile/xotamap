# WOTA — Wainwrights On The Air

## What is WOTA?

Wainwrights On The Air (WOTA) is a British amateur radio programme focused on the 214 Wainwright fells in the Lake District, plus additional Outlying Fells (544 references total). The summits are named after the renowned guidebook author Alfred Wainwright.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** Summit area
- **Equipment:** Portable, summit reached on foot
- **Operating modes:** SSB, CW, FM, all digital modes
- **Region:** UK Lake District
