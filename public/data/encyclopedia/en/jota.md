# JOTA — Jamboree On The Air

## What is JOTA?

Jamboree On The Air (JOTA) is the annual amateur radio event of the worldwide Scout movement, organised by WOSM (World Organization of the Scout Movement). Held on the third full weekend of October, it is one of the largest youth events in amateur radio, with Scout groups in over 170 countries participating.

JOTA gives Scouts the opportunity to connect with peers worldwide through amateur radio. Licensed operators provide technical support and supervise the stations.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Equipment:** Any setup at a Scout group station
- **Operating modes:** SSB, CW, FM, all digital modes
- **Period:** Third full weekend of October
- **Special:** Organised by WOSM. JOTI (Jamboree On The Internet) runs in parallel.
