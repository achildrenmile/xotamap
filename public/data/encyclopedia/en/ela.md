# ELA — English Lighthouses Awards

## What is ELA?

English Lighthouses Awards (ELA) is an amateur radio programme for lighthouses in England. Coordinated through the GMA platform, it includes approximately 200 English lighthouses.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the lighthouse
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Region:** England
