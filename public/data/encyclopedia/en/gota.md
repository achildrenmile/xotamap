# GOTA — Get On The Air

## What is GOTA?

Get On The Air (GOTA) is a special station at the annual ARRL Field Day, specifically designed for unlicensed or newly licensed operators. GOTA stations allow newcomers to make their first radio contacts under the supervision of a licensed control operator.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Equipment:** Supervised station at Field Day events
- **Operating modes:** SSB, CW, FM, all digital modes
- **Period:** Fourth full weekend of June (ARRL Field Day)
- **Special:** For unlicensed or newly licensed operators, supervised by a licensed control operator.
