# SiOTA — Silos On The Air

## What is SiOTA?

Silos On The Air (SiOTA) is an Australian amateur radio programme promoting operating from grain silos. Australia has over 700 registered silos, which are often prominent landmarks in rural areas. Many of these silos have also become famous for their Silo Art installations.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the silo
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Region:** Australia
