# ECA — English Castles Awards

## What is ECA?

English Castles Awards (ECA) is an amateur radio programme for castles in England. Coordinated through the GMA platform, it includes approximately 1,000 English castles and fortresses.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or near the castle
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Region:** England
