# KRMNPA — Keith Roget Memorial National Parks Award

## What is KRMNPA?

The Keith Roget Memorial National Parks Award (KRMNPA) is an Australian amateur radio programme for national parks in VK (Victoria/Australia). Coordinated through the ParksnPeaks platform, it includes approximately 800 references.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** Within the national park
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Region:** Australia (Victoria)
