# CHOTA — Churches & Chapels On The Air

## What is CHOTA?

Churches & Chapels On The Air (CHOTA) is an annual amateur radio event organised by WACRAL (World Association of Christian Radio Amateurs and Listeners). Held on the second Saturday of September, it promotes operating from churches and chapels.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At or in the church/chapel
- **Equipment:** Any portable or mobile setup
- **Operating modes:** SSB, CW, FM, all digital modes
- **Period:** Second Saturday of September
- **Special:** Annual event organised by WACRAL.
