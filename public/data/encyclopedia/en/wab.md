# WAB — Worked All Britain

## What is WAB?

Worked All Britain (WAB) is a British amateur radio programme based on Ordnance Survey 10km grid squares. The goal is to work or activate as many different British grid squares as possible. Over 10,000 references.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** Within the OS grid square
- **Equipment:** Any setup
- **Region:** United Kingdom
