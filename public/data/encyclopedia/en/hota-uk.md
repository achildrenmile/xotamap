# HOTA-UK — Houses On The Air

## What is HOTA-UK?

Houses On The Air (HOTA-UK) is a British amateur radio programme where operators activate registered residential addresses. Each registered house receives a HOTA reference number. The programme is expanding internationally with over 5,000 references.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Activation zone:** At the registered house address
- **Equipment:** Any setup from the house location
- **Region:** UK / International
