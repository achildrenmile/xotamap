# YOTA — Youngsters On The Air

## What is YOTA?

Youngsters On The Air (YOTA) is an international amateur radio programme for young operators under 26. Supported by IARU Region 1, YOTA promotes youth involvement in amateur radio. The annual YOTA Month takes place in December, with youth groups operating special callsigns.

## Rules

- **Minimum QSOs:** 1 contact
- **Equipment:** Any setup
- **Operating modes:** SSB, CW, FM, FT8, all digital modes
- **Period:** December (YOTA Month) with special callsigns
- **Special:** Participants must be under 26 years old.
